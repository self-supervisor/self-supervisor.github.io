Efficient Generalised Spherical CNNs

September 1, 2020

Authors: Oliver J. Cobb, Christopher G. R. Wallis, Augustine N. Mavor-Parker, Augustin Marignier, Matthew A. Price, Mayeul d’Avezac & Jason D. McEwen

Cobb et al. introduce a general theoretical framework for spherical CNNs with improved computational efficiency. Currently under review at ICLR 2021.
